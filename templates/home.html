Prisoner's Dilemma game

You will play the “Prisoner's Dilemma”, do you know this game?

In the "Prisoner's Dilemma" the two players are in different rooms and cannot communicate with each other.
For each game, each of them is given two choices: to collaborate or not to collaborate with the other player.
These two choices lead to different results, based on your choice AND the choice of the other player.

Soon you will play the “Prisoner's Dilemma” to obtain University Training Credits (CFU). The number of credits obtained by you and the other player depends on both your game (choice: collaborate or not collaborate) and the other player's game (choice: collaborate or not collaborate).
Each CFU is worth 25 hours. Here you will play based on this table which shows the number of hours that you can obtain to obtain CFUs.
